# Code of Conduct

## 0. Don't be a dick.

That's it. That's the rule.

If you're unsure whether something crosses the line, it probably does.
If someone tells you it does, listen.

---

## What this means (non-exhaustive)

* Be respectful of people's time, work, and boundaries.
* Critique code, not humans.
* Assume good intent, but don't weaponize it.
* No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
* No ego contests. This is not the place.

---

## What happens if you ignore this

Maintainers reserve the right to:

* Remove comments, issues, or PRs
* Lock threads
* Ban contributors

No drama, no debate club, no second-by-second arbitration.

---

We're here to build something useful.
Act like someone others would want to collaborate with.

Or don't participate.
